Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Spark Play Games, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Automatically Collected Information: We may collect basic usage analytics such as page views, game plays, and device information to improve our service.
  • Contact Information: When you contact us through our contact form, we collect your name and email address only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for website functionality. No tracking cookies are used without your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our gaming platform
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our game selection

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • To trusted service providers who assist in operating our website (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • With your explicit consent

4. Game Content and Third-Party Services

Our games are embedded from trusted third-party sources. These games may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns about data collection within specific games.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our website is designed to be family-friendly and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Our games are suitable for all ages, and we take extra care to ensure a safe gaming environment for younger users.

7. Third-Party Services and Vendors

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): For website analytics and user behavior tracking
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Game Providers: Third-party game hosting services for embedded games

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Google processes data.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content recommendations
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our Cookie Settings banner. Disabling cookies may limit some website functionality.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request data portability

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@sparkplaygames.com
  • Contact Form: sparkplaygames.com/contact
